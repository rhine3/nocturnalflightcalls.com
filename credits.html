Acknowledgements

This website is a collaboration between the Listening Together Project, the Kitzes Lab at the University of Pittsburgh, and the Phil Taylor Lab at Acadia University:

  • Our initial dataset of flight calls were collected through a series of nocturnal monitoring stations run by Phil Taylor (Arcadia University) and John Kearney (Listening Together Project)
  • John Kearney identified and annotated the calls on this webpage
  • Annotation dataset was standardized and compiled by Lauren Chronister, Louis Freeland-Haynes, and Tessa Rhinehart (University of Pittsburgh)
  • Tessa Rhinehart prepared the website content and designed and built the website

This website's header image is a compilation of photos by Geoffrey Gilmour-Taylor ("Oriole in Flight", CC BY-NC-SA 2.0) and Catherine Singleton ("Moon", CC BY-SA 2.0).
